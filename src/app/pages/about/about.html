ABOUT

CA Sunil D. Naik

CA Sunil D. Naik is a qualified Chartered Accountant with a distinguished professional standing of over 44 years. He is also qualified as a Company Secretary, Cost Accountant, and Arbitrator.

He is an All India Rank Holder in both the Chartered Accountancy and Company Secretary examinations, reflecting exceptional academic and professional excellence.

CA Sunil D. Naik has an established practice providing comprehensive professional services in Audit, Income Tax, Goods & Services Tax (GST), RERA, Project Financing, and Arbitration assignments. He specializes in delivering offshore accounting and audit support services tailored for CPAs and accounting firms, enabling global clients to enhance efficiency and maintain regulatory compliance.

Supported by a team of qualified professionals, the firm delivers accurate, timely, and compliant solutions, including bookkeeping, tax preparation, and audit documentation. Acting as an extended arm of client practices, the firm helps organizations scale efficiently, reduce operational overheads, and focus on high-value advisory and strategic services.
